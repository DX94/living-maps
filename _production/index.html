  • About

LivingCities visualization

Cities are a fundamental part of everybody's lives, but at Here we also see they have their own life. With this visualization we want to inspire developers to take a look at data APIs and CartoDB technology to develop great visualizations on cities.

  • About the data

    The data behind this visualization is collected by HERE continuously from connected devices. We do not collect personal data, we collect small data changes to know how is the traffic in the city, if a road is closed, is a new road is open, etc. This is how we construct our fresh maps that are used online and in so many devices.

  • About the visualization

    We believe this data can be used for many different things and we are partnering with CartoDB to look for interesting visualizations that tell us different stories. In this visualization you can see traffic density over maps and lot of facts derived from the aggregated vision we get from the city.

  • Technology

    The visualization has been built by CartoDB team using their cloud infrastructure. It make use of the HTML5 Torque technology to animate geospatial datasets over time. Follow the CartoDB blog over the next days to learn more about how specifically this has been done.
