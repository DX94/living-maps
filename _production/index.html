About LivingCities

Cities are a fundamental part of everybody’s lives, but at Here we also see they have their own life.

Technology

The visualization has been built by CartoDB team using their cloud infrastructure and their Torque technology to animate geospatial datasets over time.

The data

The data behind this visualization is collected by HERE continuously from connected devices. We do not collect personal data, we collect small data changes to know how is the traffic in the city, if a road is closed, is a new road is open, etc.

The visualization

We believe this data can be used for many different things and we are partnering with CartoDB to look for interesting visualizations that tell us different stories. In this visualization you can see traffic density over maps and lot of facts derived from the aggregated vision we get from the city.
