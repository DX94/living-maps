LivingCities

A joint experiment by HERE and CartoDB

At HERE we collect millions of GPS traces to build our always updated maps. This data help us to give you better directions and find the closest shop. But this data also says a lot about he cities where we lived. We have partnered with visualization experts CartoDB to show you some facts about 5 cities and we are inviting everybody to join us in a challenge to make your own visualizations.
